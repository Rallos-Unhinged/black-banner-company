Core Concept

Persistent Characters

Your character continues to grow across contracts, downtime, and server events.

Contract-Based Gameplay

Adventures are posted as contracts, allowing players to sign up and play at different times.

Rank-Based Progression

Your level determines your rank, which helps balance contracts, rewards, and item access.

Meaningful Consequences

Success and failure both matter. The world responds to what players do.

Earn your place. Prove your worth. Rise through the ranks.

Contract Rules

Players may take contracts one rank above or below their own.

For contracts lower than the player's rank, they earn Gold and Work Hours, but do not earn PP.

Attempting contracts above your rank is dangerous and not recommended.

Work Hours

After each completed contract, players earn 32 Work Hours.

Used For

Training and crafting.

Spend Before Next Contract

Work Hours must be spent before your next contract.

Unused Hours

Unused hours are lost.

Crafting

Use Work Hours with the crafting guide.

Contract Failure & World Consequences

Failure matters.

Possible Losses

  • No gold
  • No PP
  • No loot or components

World Response

  • Enemy factions gain ground
  • Regions become more dangerous
  • Company reputation may suffer
The world remembers.

Item Masters, Crafting & The Company Vault

Commission Items

Company Vault

Player Trade

Trade gold, items, or components with other players.

Contract Posting & Sign-Ups

DMs will post contracts including the contract rank, player limit, and a brief description.

Players may freely sign up. Priority goes to players of the intended rank.

Final Words

The Black Banner Company is built on fair progression, meaningful risk, persistent character growth, and respect for DM balance and effort.

Choose your contracts wisely. Earn your banner. Rise through the ranks.
