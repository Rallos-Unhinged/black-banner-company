Core Concept

Persistent Characters

Your character continues to grow across contracts, downtime, and server events.

Contract-Based Gameplay

Adventures are posted as contracts, allowing players to sign up and play at different times.

Rank-Based Progression

Your level determines your rank, which helps balance contracts, rewards, and item access.

Meaningful Consequences

Success and failure both matter. The world responds to what players do.

Earn your place. Prove your worth. Rise through the ranks.

Contract Rules

Players may take contracts one rank above or below their own. For example, D Rank players may take E, D, or C Rank contracts.

For contracts lower than the player's rank, they earn Gold and Work Hours, but do not earn PP.

This rule exists to prevent high-rank characters from trivializing low-rank content and to prevent low-rank characters from entering deadly contracts. Attempting contracts above your rank is dangerous and not recommended.

Magic Item Restrictions

To keep the server balanced, magic item usage is rank-locked. Loot and usage must always match your current rank.

Work Hours

After each completed contract, players earn 32 Work Hours.

Used For

Training and crafting.

Spend Before Next Contract

Work Hours must be spent before your next contract.

Unused Hours

Unused hours are lost. Plan wisely.

Crafting

Use Work Hours with the crafting guide to create items, train, or prepare for future contracts.

Contract Failure & World Consequences

Failure matters.

Possible Contract Losses

  • No gold
  • No Progress Points
  • No loot or components, at DM discretion

World Response

  • Enemy factions gain ground
  • Regions become more dangerous
  • Future contracts increase in difficulty
  • Company reputation may suffer
The world remembers.

Item Masters, Crafting & The Company Vault

Gold earned through contracts can be used to:

Commission Items

Commission magic items from Item Masters.

Craft Gear

Craft items using earned components.

Company Vault

Purchase materials from the Company Vault.

Player Trade

Trade gold, items, or components with other players.

Item Masters are DMs entrusted with crafting and balance oversight.

Contract Posting & Sign-Ups

DMs will post contracts including the contract rank, player limit, and a brief description.

Players may freely sign up. Priority goes to players of the intended rank. Extra sign-ups are waitlisted and may be called in if needed.

Final Words

The Black Banner Company is built on fair progression, meaningful risk, persistent character growth, and respect for DM balance and effort.

Choose your contracts wisely. Earn your banner. Rise through the ranks.
