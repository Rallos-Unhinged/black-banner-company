What Is The Black Banner Company?

The Black Banner Company is a mercenary guild operating from the city of Drathmere in the land of Thracia. Players take on contracts ranging from monster hunts and investigations to political intrigue, dangerous expeditions, and server-wide events that shape the future of the world.

Instead of following a single campaign, the server runs as a persistent shared world where player choices matter. Every contract can leave a mark. Every character can build a reputation. Every victory, failure, bargain, and discovery becomes part of the Company's history.
