What is a Bastion? Overview

A Bastion is a location that belongs to your character — a base, stronghold, or hideout that you build over time. It offers a refuge from adventuring and provides opportunities to craft items, harvest resources, build vehicles, and gain buffs for future quests.

Your Bastion is also a creative space — give your hirelings personalities, design a floor plan, and weave it into your character's story. Up to six players can combine their Bastions to share facilities.

When Can I Start?Build your first basic facility at D-Rank (Level 6). Special facilities unlock at C-Rank (Level 9).

Building & Costs Core Rules

Construction Rules• You can only build one facility at a time.
• Every time you reset your workweek, you also get a Construction Turn.
• Facilities become operational at the start of the turn after they complete.
• All Basic Facilities start as Cramped. You cannot skip sizes.

Bastion Turns Core Rules

Two Types of TurnsMonthly Bastion Turn: Happens on the 1st of each real-life month.

Individual Bastion Turn: Happens when your DM calls for it during a quest.
OrdersOnce per Individual Bastion Turn, you can issue one order to each applicable facility.

Combined Bastions Core Rules

  • Up to six players can combine their Bastions.
  • You can benefit from passive bonuses of other players' facilities.
  • Orders only affect you and your own Bastion.
  • Defenders can be deployed to help allies during attacks or sieges.

Defending Your Bastion Combat

You cannot be attacked until you have at least four facilities.

How Bastion Combat WorksEach attacker and Defender rolls their combat die. A Defender dies if they roll 3 or lower on a d6, but still kills their attacker.

Basic Facilities Facilities

Basic facilities are available to all Bastions starting at D-Rank. You can have one of every Basic Facility type.

Barracks

Barracks
BasicD-Rank Start

Sleeping quarters for your Bastion Defenders. Order Recruit: Recruit up to 4 Bastion Defenders.

Bedroom

Long resting in your Bastion grants 1d4 + Con Modifier Max HP, plus 1d4 per rank above D.

Battlements

Walls and archers. Must be the same rank as you to function. Can repel attackers and reduce Defender death thresholds.

Dining Room

Dining here grants 1d4 + Con Modifier Temporary HP, plus 1d4 per rank above D.

Kitchen

Grants Advantage on cooking treat crafts of the appropriate rank. Chef's Expertise applies if you have the Chef Feat.

Parlor

Order Parle: A short rest in the Parlor grants Heroic Inspiration when requirements are met.

Shop

Required to sell goods in the server's Market channel.

Storage

Store equipment while adventuring. Can only contain items equal to or lower than its own rank.

Well Room

Reliable drinking water for your Bastion. Helps keep facilities running during a siege.

Harvestarium

Choose a specialization to harvest extra materials when you earn Rewards.

Humble Exterior

Your Bastion looks unimportant. Surprise Attack and Siege events are treated as Nothing Happens for you if requirements are met.

Rank C — Special Facilities

Armory

Improves Defender survivability with To Arms! and To the Walls!

Library

Research topics and reduce certain crafting costs.

Sanctuary

Craft Blessed Medallions and perform Ceremony.

Stables

House mounts, harden steeds, and retrieve monster hunt bodies.

Storehouse

Premium Procurement for nonmagical items.

Workshop

Choose one tool or kit and gain Expertise while using this facility.

Rank B — Special Facilities

Naval Bastion Fittings

Move your Bastion to the high seas.

Gaming Hall

Gamble and roll on winnings tables.

Graveyard

Bury and raise dead Defenders.

Laboratory

Supports Alchemist, Herbalism, or Brewer crafts.

Sacristy

Holy Water and Divine Acknowledgement.

Siege Workshop

Build and operate Ballistas, Catapults, and Cannons.

Teleportation Circle

Build a permanent teleportation circle over repeated orders.

Theatre

Prepare shows and earn Theater Dice.

Training Area

Train with Battle Experts, Marksmen, and other instructors.

Museum

Research lore and create lucky charms.

Rank A — Special Facilities

Archive

Helpful Lore and Reference Books.

Meditation Chamber

Cure phobias, madness, or insanity.

Menagerie

House and tame beasts.

Observatory

Contact Other Plane charms and Augury.

Pub

Pub Specials treated as Very Rare potions.

Greater Restoration charm and Arcane Talisman.
